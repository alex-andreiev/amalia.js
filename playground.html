amalia.js-player

Metadata-enriched HTML5 video player — a maintained fork of INA's amalia.js. This interactive playground configures the player live: change the parameters on the right and they apply instantly, with the generated $("#player").mediaPlayer(config) shown below. It also demonstrates the extra control-bar widgets — subtitles, download, and playlist.
